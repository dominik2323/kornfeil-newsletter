Principle

The BioTherm thermo-oil boiler is an eco-friendly alternative that uses residual natural material instead of fossil fuels to heat thermo-oil ovens. You can heat the boiler with wood chips or pellets, for example.

Advantages

Cheaper energy sources

To heat the thermo-oil ovens we can use wood chips or pellets, which are orders of magnitude cheaper than fossil fuels

Automatic boiler cleaning

Forget about disposing of residual waste, the BioTherm boiler does it all for you.

Controls

The clear touch screen display helps you to set all parameters and monitor the combustion process and operating indicators.

Compatibility with EkoBlok

When installed together with the EkoBlok, you will recover 20-30% of your input energy. You can use these heat sources for heating or cooling purposes, for example.
